Hobbies

I crochet because it allows you to quiet your mind and think through processes. I've worked myself through a lot of problems in giving deeper thought to finally put ongoing issues that bounce in my head. It helps work through your problems. I also do it because being able to make something useful from a ball of yarn, crochet hook and a pattern feels good! It's rewarding to do and you're able to make things for you and your home as well as gifts for your friends and family.
